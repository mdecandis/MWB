FAQ

What’s included in my PDF blueprint?

Your personalized PDF includes a supplement routine, nutrition guidance, movement suggestions, and a clear daily structure — all tailored to your needs. It’s designed to help you build momentum, stay consistent, and move toward the version of wellness that matters to you.

Is this plan designed by a doctor or certified nutritionist?

No, and that’s intentional. These plans are crafted using a blend of research-backed strategies, artificial intelligence, real-life application, and human insight. We don’t claim to replace medical advice — instead, we offer clear, practical guidance that helps you take daily ownership of your wellness in a way that feels realistic and sustainable.

How long does it take to see results?

Every person’s pace is different, but most people notice early shifts in energy, sleep, digestion, or clarity in the first few weeks. The real goal isn’t a quick fix, it’s consistent, measurable progress that evolves over time.

Do I need recent lab work to get started?

You don’t need labs to get started. We build your plan based on your intake form — including your goals, habits, and any health background you choose to share. If you do have labs, we offer an optional upgrade to layer them in for more targeted personalization.

What happens after I buy?

After purchase, you’ll be taken to a short intake form. Once that’s submitted, we create your plan and deliver your personalized P DF by email within a few days. If you’re on the monthly plan, we’ll follow up every 4th week with a new monthly blueprint designed to fine tune your strategy.

Can I use this plan if I’m already working with a coach or doctor?

Definitely. Your plan is meant to work with whatever you’re already doing, not against it. It can even help you have better, more focused conversations with your doctor. .
